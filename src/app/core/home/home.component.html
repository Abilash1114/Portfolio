My Creative Identity

I’m Abilash, A Passionate UI/UX Designer and Frontend Developer with 3 years of experience crafting intuitive and visually engaging digital experiences. My work centers on Userc Centered Design principle (UCD), focusing on creating seamless, accessible, and engaging interfaces that enhance user satisfaction. I specialize in Figma, Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator to design clean, modern and user friendly interfaces. Alongside design, I bring ideas to life through Angular for web and Flutter for mobile, ensuring a perfect balance between design and functionality. I’ve contributed to several projects, including e-Commerce platforms and innovative web applications, delivering experiences that align with business goals and user needs. I stay current with new design system practices and interface design trends to build meaningful and effective digital products.
