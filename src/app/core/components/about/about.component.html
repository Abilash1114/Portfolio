About Me

My Creative Identity

I'm Abilash, a passionate UI/UX Designer and Frontend Developer with 3 years of experience crafting intuitive and visually engaging digital experiences. My work centres on User-Centred Design (UCD) principles — creating seamless, accessible, and engaging interfaces that enhance user satisfaction. I specialise in Figma, Adobe Photoshop, and Illustrator to design clean, modern, and user-friendly interfaces. Alongside design, I bring ideas to life through Angular for web and Flutter for mobile, ensuring a perfect balance between design and functionality. I've contributed to several projects including e-commerce platforms and innovative web applications, delivering experiences that align with business goals and user needs.
