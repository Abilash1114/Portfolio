About Me

My Creative Identity

I'm Abilash, a UI/UX Designer and Frontend Developer with 3+ years of experience building intuitive, visually engaging digital experiences. I work across FinTech, SaaS, and eCommerce — designing interfaces that are clean, accessible, and aligned with both user needs and business goals. My process is rooted in User-Centred Design (UCD), backed by data-driven decisions and A/B testing that have driven 30% performance improvements and reduced user drop-off rates. On the design side, I specialise in Figma, Adobe Photoshop, and Illustrator. On the development side, I build with Angular 17 for web and Flutter for mobile. This dual expertise lets me spot and fix friction points across both design and code — directly reducing drop-off rates and bounce rates that most designers can't trace to their root cause. I also ensure every product meets WCAG 2.1 accessibility standards, building experiences that are inclusive by default
